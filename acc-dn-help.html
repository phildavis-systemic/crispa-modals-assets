Identifying and Entering the Document Number

Before entering your document number, you have to remove:

  • any special characters such as brackets, parentheses, hyphens, dots or spaces; and
  • the words 'No:', 'Vol', 'Page No', 'Client Id:' and 'Evidence No:' if they appear.

Important: Depending on the document's type and age, the number may be located on the rear of the document.
