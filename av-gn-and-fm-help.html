Given and Family Names Help Modal

Given and Family Names Help

Enter your given names exactly as shown (inc. middle names). If nothing is shown, leave the field blank.

Enter your family name exactly as shown on your passport.

Names shown using Accented or International Characters

If your names on your passport are shown using accented, international characters or anything other than standard English alphabetic characters, you must enter your names as shown at the bottom of your passport page. See the example below.

Applicants with Single Name Passports

If your passport only shows a single name (i.e. there are no given names or no family name), the single name must be entered in the Family Name field regardless of where it appears on the passport. The Given Names field should be left blank.
